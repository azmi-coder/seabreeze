About Seabreeze

Discover the story behind our Mediterranean Paradise

Our story

Seabreeze Coastal Retreat is a product of a dream, which was to establish an oasis of the embodiment of modern luxury and natural beauty along the immaculate beaches of the Mediterranean coast. We started or trip in 2015 when we learnt about this beautiful place and imagined our own space where a person could forget the busy routine of their everyday existence and get in touch with the peaceful beat of the ocean.

All of our details of our guesthouse were worked out in minimalist elegance of living on the coast. Our architecture is clean and the color scheme we use is calmed with sea and sand colors which have in turn created a relaxed atmosphere and helped in rejuvenating.

We do not just offer beautiful accommodations. We support sustainable tourism and we collaborate well with our local community to make sure that out existence will not interfere with but also enhance the natural beauty of our coastal home. We are locally sourced, favor local artisans and ensure that we do not have a significant environmental footprint.

Our Philosophy

To us, simplicity is the only way to achieve true luxury at Seabreeze. Our culture resolves around giving the guest what they need to have the best vacation: breathtaking scenes, relaxing areas, and the ability to relax at their own time. You can find some adventure on the coast of relaxing moments of thought and our retreat is your ideal home base.
